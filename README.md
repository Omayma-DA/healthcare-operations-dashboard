# Healthcare Operations Dashboard
Power BI project for evaluating hospital operations data, including admissions, departments, trends, and wait times.

## Goal
USe healthcare data to:
- Track operational performance
- Identify trends in patient visits
- Support  data-driven decision making

## Dataset
- Simulated ER dataset with 5,000 patient visits
- Includes visit dates, urgency levels, wait times, outcomes, and satisfaction scores

## Dashboard Features

## Executive Overview
- KPI cards for total patients, average wait time, and satisfaction
- Trend Analysis of patient visits over time
- Breakdown of patients by urgency level

## Oprational Breakdown
- patient visits by time of day
- Distribution of patient outcomes

## Key Insights
- Patient visits fluctuate throughout the year, peaking in late summer and early fall
- Patient distribution is relatively balanced across urgecy levels
- A significant portion of patients areadmitted, indicating many cases require further treaments
- Most visits occur during afternoon and evening hours
